## Who We Are

The **Quantum Computing Society of the Philippines (QCSP)**, also known as **OneQuantum Philippines**, is a non-profit community dedicated to building the country’s quantum future. Founded in 2017, QCSP brings together **students, educators, researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers** who are passionate about **quantum computing and quantum information science and technology (QIST)**.

We work to **raise awareness**, **develop local talent**, and **build real-world applications** of quantum technologies for the Philippines. Through **lecture series, hackathons, workshops, conferences, and partnerships** with government, academia, and industry, QCSP helps Filipinos explore how quantum technologies can support **national development, innovation, and responsible adoption**.

---

## What We Do

- **Education & Training**  
  Introductory and advanced sessions on quantum computing and QIST for students, faculty, and professionals.

- **Hackathons & Competitions**  
  Hands-on events where participants design and implement quantum-inspired and quantum-based solutions.

- **Community Building**  
  A national network of chapters and partners across universities, industry, and government agencies.

- **Policy & Ecosystem Support**  
  Engagements that help align the Philippine quantum ecosystem with global best practices and opportunities.

Our vision is to be a global force for good in Quantum Tech and support humanity's transition into the quantum era in an inclusive way, enabling everyone to contribute, grow and make a positive impact.

Our mission it to accomplish this by providing a global community with local chapters, professional mentoring, career opportunities and quantum tech skills.
